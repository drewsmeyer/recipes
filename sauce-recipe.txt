Ingredients:
- Two 12oz cans Contadina brand tomato paste
- One 15oz can Hunts brand tomato sauce
- 4-5 large garlic cloves (peeled)
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 tablespoons dried parsley flakes
- 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt 

Directions:
- Put tomato sauce and tomato paste, along with 4 cans of water
from paste cans, into a large pot.
- Add remaining ingredients.
- Heat on low and stir frequently until paste blends in and sauce starts to
simmer.
- Add meatballs, or whatever meat you are using.
- Bring back to a simmer and simmer for at least 3 hours.
